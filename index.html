§ 01 · About

An engineer working at the seam where hardware meets intelligence.

I'm a Robotics and Automation engineer with a practical, full-stack approach — I enjoy owning a problem from CAD and mechanical integration all the way through firmware, perception, and control.

My interests live at the boundary of mechatronics and software: closed-loop control, sensor fusion, embedded systems, and industrial automation. I care about reliability, calibration, and the unglamorous details that decide whether a robot works on demo day or in a factory at 3 a.m.

Outside of engineering, I read about systems thinking, tinker with personal electronics projects, and spend too much time reverse-engineering off-the-shelf hardware.
